iPhone + Mac: Best Experience

You have the optimal setup! Your messages sync directly to your Mac via iCloud.

Feature Available
Auto sync ‚úÖ Yes - Desmond automates this
Reactions & effects ‚úÖ Full support
Data freshness ‚úÖ Real-time
1
Download Desmond

Clone or download the repository:

git clone https://github.com/christreadaway/desmond.git cd desmond

Or download as ZIP

2
Grant Permissions

Open System Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security and add Terminal to:

  • Full Disk Access (required)
  • Accessibility (for sync automation)

For Contacts access, run this command:

osascript -e 'tell application "Contacts" to get name of first person'

Click OK when prompted, then restart Terminal.

3
Sync Your Messages (if needed)

If your iCloud Messages sync keeps pausing, let Desmond handle it:

chmod +x desmond.sh ./desmond.sh

Desmond will click "Sync Now" every 15 seconds until complete.

4
Export Your Messages
python3 imessage_exporter.py --full

Your messages will be exported to: ~/Downloads/iMessages_Export/

2
Download Desmond

Download Desmond as ZIP and extract it.

3
Create an iPhone Backup
  1. Connect your iPhone to your PC
  2. Open iTunes (Win 10) or Apple Devices (Win 11)
  3. Select your iPhone
  4. Uncheck "Encrypt local backup"
  5. Click "Back Up Now"
‚ö†Ô∏è Important

Encrypted backups cannot be read. Make sure encryption is OFF.

4
Export Your Messages

Double-click desmond_windows.bat or run:

Android + Mac

You'll use the "SMS Backup & Restore" app to create a backup, then export it.

Feature Available
SMS/MMS ‚úÖ Full support
Call logs ‚úÖ Included
Reactions ‚ùå Not available on Android
1
Install SMS Backup & Restore

Download from Google Play Store:

SMS Backup & Restore

2
Create a Backup
  1. Open the app and tap Backup Now
  2. Select Messages (and optionally Call Logs)
  3. Choose where to save (Google Drive or local storage)
  4. Wait for backup to complete
üí° Tip

Enable "Include contact names" in backup settings for better exports.

3
Transfer to Mac

Get the .xml backup file onto your Mac:

  • Download from Google Drive, or
  • Transfer via USB cable, or
  • Email it to yourself

Put it in your Downloads folder for easy detection.

4
Download Desmond
git clone https://github.com/christreadaway/desmond.git cd desmond
5
Export Your Messages
python3 android_sms_exporter.py

The script will automatically find your backup file. Or specify it directly:

python3 android_sms_exporter.py --file ~/Downloads/sms-backup.xml

Your messages will be exported to: ~/Downloads/Android_SMS_Export/

üéâ That's it!
